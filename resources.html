COMPUTER SCIENCE RESOURCES IN CHICAGO

Our Slack Channel

Slack is a messaging service that allows for groups of people to get together and collaborate. Our Slack is an amazing resource filled with dozen of your peers, both graduated and non-graduated, who are happy to help you with any questions or problems you might be facing in your classes, personal projects, or anything in between. We aim to be an amazing resource and a helpful commmunity of Computer Scientists right here at Loyola. To join please send an email to dontpanic.luc@gmail.com and we would be more than happy to have you!

1871

1871 is a tech incubator (a tech incubator is a place where startups can go to try and receive funding) located downtown in the Merchandise Mart. They host multiple free events each month that are open to the public and great for students. Loyola has a connection to 1871 through Loyola's Tech incubator, called Ignite Labs, where you can use 1871 as a workspace for free if you have a start up of your own. Learn more about how Ignite Labs and 1871 here.

Built in Chicago

Built in Chicago is an amazing website that lists many important Computer Science events happening in Chicago. It is a hub for local startups and all things tech. It is definitely worth your time to go through their events page.
